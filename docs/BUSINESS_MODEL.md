# 🚀 Buddi: Business Model Strategy

## 1. Executive Summary
**Buddi** is an AI-driven clinical workflow intelligence platform designed to alleviate the massive administrative burden on healthcare providers. By leveraging agentic AI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Buddi transforms passive Electronic Health Records (EHR) into active, automated workflows—starting with Prior Authorization, Patient Briefing, and Risk Management.

---

## 2. The Problem
- **Clinician Burnout**: Doctors spend up to 2 hours on paperwork for every 1 hour of patient care.
- **Administrative Friction**: Prior authorizations are manual, error-prone, and lead to delayed patient care.
- **Data Overload**: Critical patient risks are often buried in hundreds of pages of messy EHR notes.
- **Fragmented Workflows**: Existing tools are silos that don't "act" on behalf of the clinician.

## 3. The Solution (Value Proposition)
Buddi provides a **Clinical Brain** that orchestrates complex medical tasks:
- **Automated Prior Auth**: Reduces manual entry from 20 minutes to seconds.
- **Pre-Visit Intelligence**: Summarizes patient risks and missing labs before the doctor enters the room.
- **Grounding & Safety**: Unlike generic AI, Buddi is grounded in ACC/AHA, ADA, and other clinical guidelines, ensuring all suggestions are evidence-based.
- **Premium Experience**: A "Mission Control" dashboard that feels like a modern SaaS tool, not legacy medical software.

---

## 4. Market Analysis
### Target Segments
1. **Mid-Market Clinics (Primary Care & Specialty)**: 5–50 provider groups that lack the deep administrative staff of large hospitals.
2. **Telehealth Platforms**: Digital-first health companies needing AI automation for their providers.
3. **Medical Billing/Admin Groups**: Companies that handle back-office work for doctors and want to increase their efficiency.

### Competitive Advantage
- **Agentic vs. Assistive**: Buddi doesn't just write text; it uses tools (EHR readers, guideline scrapers) to perform actions.
- **Shadow Mode**: A unique "training" mode that allows providers to build trust in the AI before full deployment.
- **HIPAA-First Architecture**: Built with isolation and auditability from day one (NemoClaw sandboxing).

---

| Tier | Pricing Model | Features |
|------|---------------|----------|
| **Professional** | $299 / Provider / Month | Core Chat, Patient Briefing, Guidelines RAG. |
| **Enterprise** | $749 / Provider / Month | Prior Auth Automation, Team Risk Dashboard, EHR Integration. |
| **Performance** | $15 / Prior Auth Case | Pay-as-you-go model for high-volume authorization needs. |

---

## 6. Go-To-Market Strategy
1. **Phase 1: Beachhead (Q3-Q4 2024)**: Focused pilot programs with 5 specialized cardiology and primary care clinics to refine UI/UX and safety layers.
2. **Phase 2: Integration (Q1-Q2 2025)**: Launch on EHR marketplaces (Athenahealth, DrChrono) to enable one-click data syncing.
3. **Phase 3: Scale (Q3 2025+)**: Partner with Managed Care Organizations (MCOs) to automate their incoming authorization pipelines.

---

## 7. Cost Structure
- **Technical**: LLM API costs (DeepSeek/MedGemma), GPU hosting for local inference, Vector Database (FAISS/Pinecone).
- **Compliance**: Bi-annual HIPAA audits and HITRUST certification preparation ($50k - $100k/year).
- **Sales**: Direct B2B sales force and medical conference presence.

---

## 8. Key Metrics for Success
- **Time Savings**: Average minutes saved per patient visit (Target: 15+ mins).
- **Auth Approval Rate**: Percentage of AI-generated Prior Auths approved on first try.
- **Clinician NPS**: Net Promoter Score among doctors (Target: >70).
- **Data Integrity**: Zero "grounding failures" where AI hallucinated a clinical guideline.

---

## 9. Regulatory & Milestones
- **Status**: Currently an "Internal Development" prototype.
- **Milestone 1**: HIPAA-compliant cloud deployment (Azure/AWS GovCloud).
- **Milestone 2**: SOC2 Type II Certification.
- **Milestone 3**: FDA Class I/II Medical Device Data System (MDDS) verification if required.
